the lineage

One continuous thread, start to finish

Every stage is its own record, linked to the one before it — so a fruiting tub doesn't just show its own history, it shows everything it came from.

01Genetics

The original sample — a print, a clone, a spore vial. Kept private; it's the root everything else traces back to.

02Inoculation

Agar, spore syringe, spore print, or liquid culture — started from your genetics, or propagated from an earlier inoculation.

03Spawn

Grain colonized from an inoculation. Log temp, humidity, and photos while it works.

04Fruiting

Spawn transferred to a bulk substrate and chamber. Pins, first flush, whatever comes next — it's all one page.

05Flush

Weigh the harvest, log the flush number, and the yield rolls up automatically.
